Chunky Clams and Pasta

Prep Time: 10 minutes                Cook Time: 30 minutes                   Total Time: 40 minutes
Ingredients
  • 1 19 oz can Chunky's New England clam chowder
  • 1/4 tsp garlic
  • 2 cups cooked spaghetti
Directions
  • Making the pasta
    1. In a saucepan, combine soup and garlic
    2. Add spaghetti
    3. Cook on medium heat and stir thoroughly
    4. Serve warm
