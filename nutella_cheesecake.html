Nutella Cheesecake

Prep Time: 30 minutes                   Cook Time: 55 minutes + 1 hour in the oven                   Total Time: 2 hours and 25 minutes
Ingredients
  • Crust
    • 30 Oreo cookies, cream removed
    • 6 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • Cream Cheese Filling
    • 35 oz cream cheese, room temperature
    • 3/4 cup heavy cream + 2 tbsp, cold
    • 2/3 cup Nutella
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1 tbsp cornstarch
    • 2 tsp vanilla extract
    • 4 eggs, room temperature
  • Nutella topping
    • 2/3 cup Nutella
    • 1/3 cup heavy cream
Directions
  • Prepare the crust
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F
    2. Remove the cream between the Oreo cookies and crush them using a food processor
    3. Melt butter and pour over crushed cookies. Process until evenly moistened
    4. Press cookie mixture with the back of the spoon into the bottom of a 10-inch springform pan
    5. Bake for 13-15 minutes. Set aside to cool completely and reduce oven to 300°F
  • Prepare the cream cheese filling
    1. In a bowl combine the sugar and cornstarch
    2. In a large bowl beat the cream cheese with 3/4 cup heavy cream until well combined and mixture seems fluffy
    3. Gradually add the sugar mixture and mix until creamy and smooth
    4. Add vinilla extract and mix again until well combined
    5. Add eggs one at a time until each egg is incorporated
    6. Reserve about 1/3 of cream cheese mixture in a separate bowl
    7. Add Nutella and 2 tbsp of heavy cream over the reserved 1/3 of the cream cheese mixture and mix until well incorporated
    8. Pour the white cream cheese mixture over the prepared crust
    9. Carefully add the Nutella mixture on top and use a rubber spatula to spread evenly
    10. Bake for 45-55 minutes on 300°F
    11. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake for another hour in the oven
    12. Remove and run a sharp knife completely around the inside edge of the pan
    13. Let it cool completely at room temperature
  • Prepare the Nutella topping
    1. Bring cream to a boil and pour over the Nutella
    2. Mix well and spread evenly over the cheesecake
    3. Cover and refrigerate overnight
    4. Add chopped hazelnuts on top before serving if desired
