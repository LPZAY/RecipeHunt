Oreo Truffles

Prep Time: 1 hour               Cook Time: N/A                 Total Time: 5 hours
Ingredients
  • 1 pkg of Oreos
  • 1 block of cream cheese (softened)
Directions
  • Making the truffles
    1. Separate the cookies from the cream
    2. Blend in a food processor until powder
    3. In a large mixing bowl, use an electric mixer to blend cream cheese
    4. With a rubber spatula, fold oreos into cream cheese until combined
    5. Roll into 1-inch balls and place on foil lined cookie sheet
    6. Cool for 4 hours in the fridge
