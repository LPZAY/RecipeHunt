Zucchini Tots

Prep Time: 25 minutes                Cook Time: 25 minutes                  Total Time: 50 minutes
Ingredients
  • 4 medium size zucchinis
  • salt
  • 3/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 3/4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp garlic
Directions
  • Making the tots
    1. Grate zucchinis into a bowl and salt
    2. Let sit 20 minutes
    3. Place into a clean dish towel and wring out moisture
    4. Put zucchinis back into bowl and add breadcrumbs, eggs, cheese, and garlic. Mix well
    5. Use tbsp to roll into tot sizes on greased cookie sheet
    6. Bake on 400Â°F for 20-25 minutes
