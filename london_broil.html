London Broil

Prep Time: 12-18 hours                  Cook Time: 25-30 minutes                   Total Time minimum: 12 hours and 25 minutes
Ingredients
  • London broil
  • Italian dressing
Directions
  • Prepare the London broil
    1. Place meat in water tight container or zip-lock bag
    2. Pour entire bottle of dressing into container or bag
    3. Marinate 12-18 hours, flipping twice
    4. Place London broil on foil lined cookie sheet, put top rack onto middle holder, and turn oven onto broil (Hi)
    5. Broil for 20 minutes on the first side, then turn and broil for about 10 more minutes(5 if you want it to be a bit more rare)
