Warm Spinach Dip

Prep Time: 5 minutes                Cook Time: 20 minutes                  Total Time: 25 minutes
Ingredients
  • 16 oz sour cream
  • 10 oz frozen box-thawed and squeezed dried spinach
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • salt & pepper to taste
Directions
  • Making the dip
    1. Blend in blender until combined
    2. Pour into 2 qt baking dish
    3. Bake on 425°F for 17-20 minutes
