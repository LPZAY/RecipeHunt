Pineapple Upside-Down Cupcakes

Prep Time: 15 minutes              Cook Time: 35 minutes               Total Time: 2 hours 50 minutes
Ingredients
  • 1 can of pineapple slices
  • 1 box of yellow or butter cake mix
  • 1 jar of maraschino cherries
  • eggs
  • butter
  • butter spray
  • 1-2 cups of brown sugar
Directions
  • Making the cupcakes
    1. Open the can of pineapples and drain the liquid into a cup or bowl and set it aside
    2. Prepare the box cake mix as usual but instead of using water use the pineapple juice drained from the can
    3. Take your muffin pan and spray it
    4. Place a about 2 tbsp of brown sugar in each holder, then place a whole pineapple slice on top
    5. Place a cherry in the middle of each pineapple slice and pour the cake batter into each holder
    6. Cook the cupcakes according the box instructions
    7. Remove the cupcakes from then pan and place on a cooling rack
    8. Let cupcakes cool for about 2 hours and repeat steps 1-7 if there is remaining batter
