Spinach Mushroom Quesadilla

Prep Time: 15 minutes                Cook Time: 6 minutes                 Total Time: 21 minutes
Ingredients
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 3 cup fresh spinach
  • salt
  • 3 eggs (scrambled)
  • 4 large tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup parmesan
Directions
  • Making the quesadilla
    1. Heat oil in frying pan
    2. Add mushrooms and garlic, cook until mushrooms look soft
    3. Remove pan and set aside
    4. Place tortilla in pan, add cheese, eggs, mushrooms and garlic, spinach, and more cheese.
    5. Fold in half
    6. Cook 6 minutes, flipping over halfway through
